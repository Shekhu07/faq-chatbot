# 🌐 Official Mutual Fund Information Sources

This document contains a curated list of official public pages from **PPFAS AMC, SEBI, and AMFI** containing Scheme Information Documents (SID), Key Information Memorandums (KIM), factsheets, riskometers, tax guides, and statement guides. 

These resources are the exact authorities our Gemini Search Grounding engine queries to retrieve facts.

---

## 📁 1. PPFAS Scheme Information Pages

*   **[Parag Parikh Flexi Cap Fund Scheme Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/mutual-funds/parag-parikh-flexi-cap-fund/)**  
    Official details, portfolio disclosures, historical performance, and direct SID/KIM download links for PPFAS's flagship flexi-cap scheme.
*   **[Parag Parikh Large Cap Fund Scheme Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/mutual-funds/parag-parikh-large-cap-fund/)**  
    Official portal for PPFAS's large cap fund launched in early 2026, detailing its focus on India's top 100 stocks.
*   **[Parag Parikh ELSS Tax Saver Fund Scheme Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/mutual-funds/parag-parikh-elss-tax-saver-fund/)**  
    Official page for the Section 80C compliant tax-saving equity fund, detailing its 3-year statutory lock-in period.
*   **[Parag Parikh Conservative Hybrid Fund Scheme Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/mutual-funds/parag-parikh-conservative-hybrid-fund/)**  
    Official information page for the conservative debt-oriented hybrid scheme, detailing asset allocation parameters.
*   **[Parag Parikh Liquid Fund Scheme Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/mutual-funds/parag-parikh-liquid-fund/)**  
    Official details for the low-risk short-term cash management scheme, including information on the Insta Redemption facility.
*   **[Parag Parikh Arbitrage Fund Scheme Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/mutual-funds/parag-parikh-arbitrage-fund/)**  
    Official portal for the arbitrage scheme, describing risk-free spreads and cash-futures yield strategies.
*   **[Parag Parikh Dynamic Asset Allocation Fund Scheme Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/mutual-funds/parag-parikh-dynamic-asset-allocation-fund/)**  
    Official page for the hybrid scheme that dynamically allocation capital across equities and debt instruments.

## 📥 2. Regulatory & Disclosure Documents (PPFAS Downloads)

*   **[PPFAS Monthly Factsheets Main Archive](https://ppfas.com/downloads/monthly-factsheets/)**  
    Official monthly disclosures detailing active portfolio weights, riskometers, benchmark returns, and expense ratios.
*   **[PPFAS Scheme Information Documents (SIDs) and KIMs Download Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/downloads/scheme-information-documents-sids-and-kims/)**  
    Complete repository of statutory offer documents (SID, KIM, and SAI) for all schemes.
*   **[PPFAS Notices and Addenda Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/downloads/notices-and-addenda/)**  
    List of official circulars, scheme modifications, fund manager changes, and pricing updates.
*   **[PPFAS Statutory Disclosures Page](https://amc.ppfas.com/downloads/statutory-disclosures/)**  
    Compliance postings, voting policies, corporate governance charts, and unclaimed dividends.
*   **[PPFAS Annual Reports Main Archive](https://amc.ppfas.com/downloads/annual-reports/)**  
    Complete collection of scheme financial audits, expense analyses, and trustee reports.
*   **[PPFAS Half-Yearly Financial Disclosures](https://amc.ppfas.com/downloads/half-yearly-financial-results/)**  
    Statutory twice-annual financial listings containing transaction tallies, management expenses, and return rates.

## 🛠️ 3. Portals, Statement Guides, & Help Desks

*   **[PPFAS SelfInvest Web Portal](https://selfinvest.ppfas.com/)**  
    The official online dashboard for investors to manage portfolios, place lump sums/SIPs, and download direct account statements.
*   **[PPFAS Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) Helpdesk](https://amc.ppfas.com/investor-desk/faqs/)**  
    Detailed answers regarding KYC registration, nominee updates, bank mandates, and statement schedules.
*   **[PPFAS Online Transaction & SIP Help Guide](https://amc.ppfas.com/investor-desk/online-transaction-guide/)**  
    Official instructions explaining how to link Net Banking, set up NACH mandates, and clear online SIP failures.
*   **[PPFAS Contact Us & Branch Locator](https://amc.ppfas.com/contact-us/)**  
    Official helpline numbers, corporate email IDs, and physical address coordinates for complaints or requests.

## 🏛️ 4. Industry-Wide Bodies (SEBI & AMFI)

*   **[AMFI Net Asset Value (NAV) Page](https://www.amfiindia.com/net-asset-value/nav-history)**  
    The official historical NAV database managed by the Association of Mutual Funds in India.
*   **[SEBI Mutual Funds Regulations & Circulars Database](https://www.sebi.gov.in/sebiweb/home/HomeAction.do?doListingAll=yes&sid=2&ssid=9&smid=0)**  
    The Securities and Exchange Board of India's directory listing mutual fund restrictions, categorization rules, and riskometer standards.
*   **[AMFI Knowledge Center for Mutual Fund Schemes](https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/knowledge-center/scheme-information)**  
    Industry guide explaining SIDs, risk metrics, lock-ins, tax advantages, and load calculations.
*   **[SEBI SCORES Grievance Redressal Portal](https://scores.sebi.gov.in/)**  
    The official platform for investors to lodge complaints against AMCs or registrars regarding statement delays or failures.
